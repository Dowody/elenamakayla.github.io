Hello There,

I'm Elena.

Currently, I’m a product designer at Microsoft. I am leading user researcher for Money in Excel, helping M365 consumers manage their personal finances. Previously at Opower, I was applying behavioral science to motivate customers to change their energy consumption at home. I redesigned the onboarding for Citibike to boost their day pass ridership. I started QNSMADE in my hometown of Queens, NYC to highlight our people and our stories. And in another life, I designed picture books and young adult novels at Penguin.

Outlook Mobile

A partner feature built within Outlook Mobile that automatically tracks mileage, time, and expenses on one’s calendar.

MileIQ

Redesigned onboarding flow of the app and personalization of the web dashboard. Launched internationally in the UK and Canada.

Citi Bike Onboarding

Redesigned the onboarding of Citi Bike, New York City’s bike share program with over 12,000 bikes across the five boroughs.

QNSMADE

Founded QNSMADE to foster community and create a digital platform to tell the diverse stories of 2+ million people who live in the NYC borough.

OPower

Redesign of the ‘Ways to Save’ product that helped PG&E and ConEd customers learn how to save energy in their homes by using behavioral science to change their energy habits.
